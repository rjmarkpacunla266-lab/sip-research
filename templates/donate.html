👋 Hi, I'm RJ Mark — I built Sturch

I'm a 14-year-old student and I built Sturch using only my mobile phone. No laptop, no computer, no mentor.

Sturch searches 250M+ academic papers from 5 databases simultaneously — completely free for students like me who can't afford paid research tools.

I built this because I know how hard it is to find good research papers for school projects. If Sturch helped your research, consider supporting me so I can keep building and eventually get a proper computer.

Every contribution goes toward keeping Sturch running and improving it for students everywhere.
