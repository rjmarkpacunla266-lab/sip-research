Search Tips

How to search like a pro

💡

Use multiple tags

Type a keyword → press Enter → add more tags → press Search. More specific tags = more relevant results.

📅

Filter by year

Use Year From and To to narrow results. For school projects, 2015–2024 is a good range.

💎

Use Load More

After results appear, tap Load More to get more papers on the same topic for free — no extra search needed.

🔖

Bookmark as you go

Don't rely on browser history. Tap Save on good papers and organize them into collections.

Rules & Disclaimer

Before you use Sturch

Don't spam the search button. Wait for results to load before searching again. Use Load More to get more papers on the same topic.

Always verify references before submitting. APA references in Sturch are auto-generated. They may have missing volume, issue, or page numbers. Double-check before academic submission.

For research assistance only. Sturch helps you find and cite papers. It does not write your research for you.

Data is from public academic databases. Sturch does not own any papers. All content belongs to their respective publishers and authors.
