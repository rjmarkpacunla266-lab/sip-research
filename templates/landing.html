Rules & Disclaimer

Before you use Sturch

Don't spam the search button. Each search uses your points. Spamming wastes your free searches and puts load on the server. Wait for results to load before searching again.

Always verify references before submitting. APA and MLA references in Sturch are auto-generated. They may contain errors. Double-check before using them in academic work.

For research assistance only. Sturch helps you find and cite papers. It does not write your research for you. Use it as a tool, not a shortcut.

Data is from public academic databases. Sturch does not own any of the papers. All content belongs to their respective publishers and authors.
